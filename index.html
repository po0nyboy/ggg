Ask the Wellbeing Wiz!

Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I spend 8 hours a day gaming. What should I do?

Try setting a timer for breaks and balancing gaming with outdoor activities. Slowly reduce your daily screen time.

Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I feel stressed from social media. Any tips?

Unfollow accounts that make you feel negative, and try a “social media detox” day each week.

Dear Wellbeing Wiz, I stay up late scrolling. How can I sleep better?

Turn off screens 1 hour before bed and replace scrolling with reading or listening to music.
